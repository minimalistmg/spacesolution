Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

Introduction

Space Solutions ("we," "us," or "our"), operating out of Karnataka, India, provides end-to-end interior design, modular furniture manufacturing, and renovation services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, interact with our Meta or Google Ads, or engage our design services.

By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy in accordance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the SPDI (Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011.

Information We Collect

To provide accurate modular designs and high-quality installations, we collect:

  • Identity & Contact Data: Full name, phone number, email address, and primary residence details.
  • Property & Project Data: Specific site addresses (in Mysore, Mandya, Madikeri, etc.), floor plans, and site photographs or videos taken during initial surveys and throughout the project duration.
  • Financial Data: GST details for billing and payment records. Note: We do not store credit card or UPI details; these are processed securely by third-party gateways.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and interaction data via the Meta Pixel or Google Ads to improve our localized marketing reach.

How We Use Your Data

We utilize your information strictly for:

  • Service Delivery: Scheduling site visits, manufacturing modular kitchen/furniture units, and managing on-site labor.
  • Marketing & Communication: Following up on Meta/Google leads via Phone, SMS, and WhatsApp regarding quotations and design updates.
  • Project Portfolio: We may use photographs of completed projects for promotional purposes (social media/website), ensuring no personal identifiable information (like family photos or specific house numbers) is visible.
  • Legal Compliance: Maintaining tax records and complying with Indian state and central regulations.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We share information only with:

  • Internal Teams & Contractors: Including factory staff, carpenters, and electricians required to complete your specific project.
  • Marketing Platforms: Meta and Google for analytics and retargeting ads within your 25-mile radius.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights in dispute resolutions.

Consent For Communication

By providing your contact details through our website, social media ads, or lead forms, you expressly consent to be contacted by Space Solutions via voice calls and SMS, WhatsApp Messenger (including automated updates), and email. This consent applies even if your number is registered on the National Do Not Call (DND) Registry.

Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures (including encrypted digital storage) to protect your project data. We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill project warranties or as required by Indian tax laws.

Your Rights & Opt-out

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your data. To opt-out of marketing communications or request data removal, please contact our Grievance Officer.
