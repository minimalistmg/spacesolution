Residential Interiors

Your home should reflect your personality. From modular kitchens to serene pooja rooms, we deliver end-to-end interior solutions.

Living Room Interiors

The Heart of Your Home

The living room is where first impressions are made. We focus on creating a balanced layout that maximizes seating without compromising on flow.

  • Custom TV units, accent walls, and ergonomic sofa layouts
  • Layered lighting for day-to-evening transitions

Modular Kitchen

Kitchens Built for Daily Use

We design kitchens that prioritize the Golden Work Triangle — the distance between your sink, stove, and refrigerator.

  • L-shaped, U-shaped, parallel, and island kitchens
  • Tandem drawers, corner carousels, and tall pantry units

Master Bedroom

Your Private Sanctuary

Your bedroom should be a retreat from the world. Our designs emphasize clutter-free surfaces and soothing color palettes.

  • King-sized bed frames with hydraulic storage
  • Upholstered headboards and integrated vanity units
